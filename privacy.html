POLICY

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TheBidCraft ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services at thebidcraft.com. By using TheBidCraft, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use TheBidCraft:

  • Account information: Your email address and password when you create an account.
  • Usage data: Number of proposals generated, features used, and subscription status.
  • Bid content: Job descriptions, client names, addresses, and other details you enter when generating proposals. This information is processed in real time to generate your proposal and is not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe. We never store your credit card details. We only receive confirmation of successful payment and your subscription status.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited — collected automatically via Google Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your TheBidCraft account
  • To generate contractor proposals using AI on your behalf
  • To process your subscription payments through Stripe
  • To send you account-related emails such as email verification and password resets
  • To provide customer support when you contact us
  • To improve our service based on how users interact with the platform
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and prevent abuse

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

3. AI-Generated Content Disclosure

TheBidCraft uses artificial intelligence — specifically the Anthropic Claude API — to generate contractor bid proposals based on the information you provide. By using this feature, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

  • The proposals generated by our AI are provided as a starting point and should be reviewed and verified by you before sending to any client.
  • AI-generated line items, pricing, and totals are estimates based on general market data. They may not reflect the exact costs, labor rates, or material prices in your area.
  • TheBidCraft is not responsible for any financial loss, incorrect pricing, or business decisions made based on AI-generated proposals. You are solely responsible for reviewing and approving any proposal before sending it.
  • The job details you enter are transmitted to Anthropic's API to generate your proposal. Anthropic processes this data in accordance with their own privacy policy. We do not store your job descriptions permanently.
  • AI-generated content should not be considered professional legal, financial, or contracting advice.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your account data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted database provider. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) on all pages
  • Secure password hashing — we never store plain-text passwords
  • Row-level security policies that ensure users can only access their own data

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no system is completely immune to security risks. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you promptly.

5. Third-Party Services

TheBidCraft uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — Account authentication and database storage. supabase.com/privacy
  • Stripe — Payment processing. stripe.com/privacy
  • Anthropic — AI proposal generation via the Claude API. anthropic.com/privacy
  • Google Analytics — Website traffic analysis. policies.google.com/privacy
  • Crisp — Live customer support chat. crisp.chat/en/privacy

6. Cookies

TheBidCraft uses cookies and local browser storage to maintain your login session and remember your preferences. We also use Google Analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our site. These are analytics-only cookies and do not track you across other websites for advertising purposes.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so may prevent you from logging in or using certain features of the service.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can update your account information at any time.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your account and all associated data by emailing us at thebidcraft@gmail.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.
  • Portability: You can request an export of your data in a readable format.

8. Children's Privacy

TheBidCraft is intended for use by adults and professional contractors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately at thebidcraft@gmail.com.
